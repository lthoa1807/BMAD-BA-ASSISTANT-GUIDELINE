# Step 1: Baseline

## Goal

Establish the business process baseline and the known capability baseline before classifying gaps.

## Instructions

Review the discovery brief or ask for equivalent context. Capture:

- Process area and scenario name
- Business outcome and criticality
- Current-state behavior
- Target standard behavior if known
- Policy, control, data, integration, and reporting constraints
- Volume, frequency, and business impact

If the target product or platform is unknown, frame fit-gap categories generically and mark product-specific validation as an open question.

## Output Fragment

Create a baseline table of process scenarios and known constraints.

## Step Documentation

Before moving to the next step, create or update `{ba_artifacts}/fit-gap/step-01-baseline-notes.md` with:

- Workflow and step name
- Inputs reviewed, including discovery artifacts or equivalent context
- Questions asked
- User responses and evidence
- Baseline scenarios with `GAP-###` IDs and upstream `DISC-###` links where available
- Decisions with `DEC-###` IDs
- Assumptions
- Risks with `RISK-###` IDs
- Open questions with `Q-###` IDs
- Output fragment
- Next action

Update `{ba_artifacts}/ba-worklog-index.md` with the completed step, artifact path, key decisions, risks, open questions, and next action.
