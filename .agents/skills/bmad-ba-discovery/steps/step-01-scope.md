# Step 1: Scope And Stakeholders

## Goal

Establish the discovery boundary, business outcomes, stakeholders, and decision context.

## Instructions

Ask for or infer the following:

- Product, platform, process, or target capability if known
- Business units, legal entities, countries, teams, sites, or shared services in scope
- Process areas in scope such as finance, procurement, sales, inventory, operations, service, HR, or reporting
- Business outcomes and success measures
- Sponsor, process owners, subject matter experts, IT owners, data owners, and compliance owners
- Timeline, implementation wave, and major constraints

Record unknowns as open questions.

## Output Fragment

Create a scope summary with stakeholders, desired outcomes, constraints, and open questions.

## Step Documentation

Before moving to the next step, create or update `{ba_artifacts}/discovery/step-01-scope-notes.md` with:

- Workflow and step name
- Inputs reviewed
- Questions asked
- User responses and evidence
- Scope findings with `DISC-###` IDs
- Decisions with `DEC-###` IDs
- Assumptions
- Risks with `RISK-###` IDs
- Open questions with `Q-###` IDs
- Output fragment
- Next action

Update `{ba_artifacts}/ba-worklog-index.md` with the completed step, artifact path, key decisions, risks, open questions, and next action.
