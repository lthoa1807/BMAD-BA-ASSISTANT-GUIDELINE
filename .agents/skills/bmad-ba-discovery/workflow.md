# BA Discovery Workflow

## Purpose

Build a concise business discovery brief before fit-gap or requirements work begins.

## Steps

1. `./steps/step-01-scope.md` - Establish scope, stakeholders, and business outcomes.
2. `./steps/step-02-current-state.md` - Capture processes, systems, data, controls, and pain points.
3. `./steps/step-03-brief.md` - Produce the business discovery brief.

## Guardrails

- Treat unknowns as open questions, not assumptions.
- Capture evidence sources for important claims.
- Distinguish core process needs from local preferences.
- Maintain step records under `{ba_artifacts}/discovery/` and update `{ba_artifacts}/ba-worklog-index.md` after each step.
- Use stable trace IDs for discovery items, decisions, risks, and open questions: `DISC-001`, `DEC-001`, `RISK-001`, and `Q-001`.
- Build the final brief from the step records, not from conversation memory alone.
