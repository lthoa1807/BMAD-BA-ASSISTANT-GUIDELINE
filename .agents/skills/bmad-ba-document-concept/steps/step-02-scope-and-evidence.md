# Step 2: Scope And Evidence

## Goal

Define document scope, boundaries, evidence needs, assumptions, and unresolved questions.

## Instructions

Clarify:

- In-scope topics, processes, systems, teams, customers, versions, or implementation waves
- Out-of-scope topics and known exclusions
- Required source evidence and source owners
- Claims that can be supported now versus claims that need confirmation
- Required approvals or stakeholder reviews
- Constraints, compliance needs, confidentiality limits, and terminology standards

If source inputs are incomplete, define what can still be drafted safely and what must remain blocked or marked as assumption.

## Output Fragment

Create a scope and evidence summary with boundaries, assumptions, questions, risks, and evidence requirements.
